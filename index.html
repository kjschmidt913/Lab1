Fast News Redesign A/B Test

About KJ

I'm a front end developer/designer and am just beginning my journey as an iOS developer. Designing, creating, and problem solving are what I love to do. I'm passionate about connecting people and building interactive content, which has made iOS so much fun to learn.

I'm currently completing all of the iOS courses included in the Udacity iOS nannodegree and then some. I'm working with Swift, Xcode, UIKit, AutoLayout, GCD, and am continuing to learn more.

On the front end side of things, I'm fluent in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Angular, and many of the accompanying technologies- git, github, terminal, node.js, jQuery, bootstrap, responsive design, API's, etc. I also have experience with email development in the SalesForce Marketing Cloud.

Outside of development work, I'm pretty handy with a camera, Final Cut Pro, Illustrator, and Photoshop.
